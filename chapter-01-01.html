Chapter One — The Parking Lot Morgan hadn’t planned on spending her lunch break in the Pep Boys parking lot cussing at a pair of windshield wipers, but here she was. One foot on the curb, sleeves shoved up, hair already losing its fight with the humidity.

The asphalt shimmered in the heat, bending the edges of the lot. The air tasted faintly of rubber and oil, the kind that clung to the back of your throat.
